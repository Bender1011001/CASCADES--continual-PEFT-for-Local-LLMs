# CASCADES v10 Research Hypotheses

This document outlines the core hypotheses for the v10 research iteration, focusing on bridging the proxy-to-generative gap, breaking the Task 1 accuracy ceiling, and resolving the 8B scaling paradox.

## 1. Generative Accuracy: The Proxy-to-EM Bridge

**Context:** v9 achieved ~36% proxy accuracy but 0% Exact Match (EM) due to the "Heretic" model's conversational default.
**Hypothesis:** The combination of **GQA-aware metric preconditioning** and **CFG decoding (λ=1.5)** will bridge this gap by enforcing structural adherence to CoT syntax while preserving reasoning depth.

- **Mechanism:** GQA preconditioning ensures K/V adapters aren't starved of gradient signal, leading to more stable attention patterns.
- **Prediction:** Generative containment (syntax adherence) will increase from ~60% to >85%, yielding the first non-zero EM scores (>5%) on the reasoning hold-out.

## 2. Task 1 Ceiling: Breaking the 17.85% Floor

**Context:** Task 1 (critical analysis/ARC) has consistently plateaued around 17-18%.
**Hypothesis:** The Task 1 floor is a result of **rank-1 FunLoRA starvation** in non-critical layers during the initial task phase.

- **Architectural Test:** Enabling **Principal Tangent Expansion** will allow the model to "revive" dead singular values in the early layers, providing the necessary representational capacity for multi-hop ARC reasoning.
- **Prediction:** Principal expansion + full v10 stack will break the 20% barrier on Task 1, reaching ~22.5% ACC.

## 3. 8B Scaling: GQA Pre-conditioning and Stability

**Context:** Llama-3-8B showed training instability and lower-than-expected gains in v9.
**Hypothesis:** The **GQA Scaling Paradox** is caused by the asymmetric gradient norm between Query and Key/Value adapters when using Grouped-Query Attention.

- **Impact Prediction:**
  - **Training Stability:** NaN loss events will be eliminated through normalized `gqa_ratio` scaling.
  - **VRAM Usage:** VRAM will remain stable at ~5.8GB for 8B models, as GQA preconditioning is a mathematical re-weighting, not a parameter increase.
  - **Performance:** 8B performance will finally surpass 4B (target: >50% ACC).

## 4. Component Contribution: The BWT King

**Hypothesis:** **Frozen Null-Space Projection (`enable_coso_nullspace`)** will yield the highest marginal gain in Backward Transfer (BWT).

- **Reasoning:** While `soft-EAR` improves training smoothness, only the strict geometric projection out of the prior-task subspace (the "frozen null-space") can fundamentally prevent the representational bleed observed in the Heretic models.
- **Prediction:** `enable_coso_nullspace` will contribute >1.5% to BWT, while other v10 patches will primarily contribute to Plasticity (ACC).
